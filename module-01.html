Anatomy & Physiology

Why this module is broken into lessons

Anatomy & physiology is dense, vocabulary-heavy theory with no real "practice" component — that's exactly the kind of content where chunking helps. It gives natural stopping points, lets you quiz-and-confirm understanding before piling on more terminology, and prevents the "wall of text" feeling that causes people to skim rather than absorb.

Suggested way to work through each lesson

  1. Read through the lesson content
  2. Explore any resources associated with that lesson
  3. Complete the quiz
  4. Listen to the podcast associated with that lesson to help summarise the content
  5. Proceed to the next lesson
